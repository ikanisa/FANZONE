FANZONE is a free football prediction app where fans predict match outcomes, earn Fan Engagement Tokens (FET), and compete on the leaderboard — no money, no gambling, just football knowledge.

🏟️ PREDICT MATCHES
Browse upcoming fixtures from the Champions League, Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A, Ligue 1, and the Malta Premier League. Pick your predictions — home win, draw, or away — and earn FET tokens when you get it right.

⚡ FAN ENGAGEMENT TOKENS (FET)
Every correct prediction earns you FET. Your token balance reflects your football knowledge over time. FET is an in-app reward — not a cryptocurrency, not real money.

📊 MATCH INSIGHTS
View AI-generated match previews, team form analysis, head-to-head records, and predicted scorelines before you make your pick. Make informed predictions backed by data.

🏆 LEADERBOARDS
Track your prediction accuracy, climb the global leaderboard, and see how you rank against other fans. Your prediction history is always available in your profile.

👤 FAN IDENTITY
Get your unique six-digit Fan ID, choose a display name, and pick your favourite team. Your profile tracks every prediction you've ever made — wins, losses, and streaks.

💰 FET WALLET
Monitor your FET balance, view your full transaction history, and see exactly how you earned each token. Your wallet is your football CV.

KEY FEATURES:
• 100% free to play — no real money, no in-app purchases
• Champions League, EPL, La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A, Ligue 1, and Malta Premier League
• AI-powered match insights and predicted scorelines
• FET token rewards for correct predictions
• Global leaderboard with Fan ID rankings
• Full prediction history and accuracy tracking
• Dark mode interface designed for match-day viewing
• WhatsApp-based login — no passwords to remember
• Works on phones and tablets
• Guest browsing with optional sign-up

FANZONE is not a betting app. There are no bookmaker odds, no real-money stakes, and no gambling mechanics. It is a free football prediction game for fans who love the sport.
