FANZONE is a sports-bar entertainment app for venues, lounges, fan zones, and hospitality operators. Guests can choose a venue in the app, order from the bar, earn Fan Engagement Tokens (FET), join curated match pools, and manage a FET wallet.

MATCH POOLS
Join curated football match pools for major competitions and venue-hosted events. Pools are entertainment only: no bookmaker odds, no real-money wagering, and no payment API.

VENUE ORDERING
Choose a participating venue, browse the menu, and place orders from the app. Customer payments happen off-platform through the venue's supported methods such as cash, MoMo/USSD instructions, or Revolut links.

FAN ENGAGEMENT TOKENS (FET)
Earn FET from venue-configured order rewards, welcome grants, qualified pool creation, invited participant rewards, and pool settlements. FET is an in-app reward, not real currency.

MATCH CONTEXT
Browse curated fixtures, team context, match status, and venue-linked pool stats for football moments selected by the platform or venue operators.

FAN IDENTITY
Get your unique Fan ID, choose a display name, and pick your favourite teams and markets.

FET WALLET
Monitor your FET balance, view your transaction history, and see exactly how each credit or debit was created.

KEY FEATURES:
• In-app venue ordering flows
• Curated match pools for major football competitions
• FET wallet with audited ledger history
• Venue-configured order rewards
• Off-platform payment handoff only
• WhatsApp-based login — no passwords to remember
• Guest browsing with optional sign-up
• Dark mode interface designed for match-day viewing
• Works on phones and tablets

FANZONE is not a betting app. There are no bookmaker odds, no real-money wagering, and no gambling mechanics.
