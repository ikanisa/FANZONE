## Full Description

FANZONE is a sports-bar entertainment and ordering app for football fans and participating venues.

Browse nearby bars, view menus, order food and drinks from your table, follow external payment instructions, and earn closed-loop FET reward points when your order qualifies.

Join venue-linked football prediction pools, create or join teams for bar games, and play centralized entertainment sessions such as Bar Trivia, Fan Trivia, Music Bingo, and Song Guess. Winners receive FET reward points only when they meet the venue order eligibility rule.

Key features:
- Browse sports bars, pubs, lounges, and restaurants.
- View menus and place venue-linked dine-in orders.
- Follow MoMo USSD, Revolut link, cash, card, or venue payment instructions.
- Use WhatsApp OTP login with no email or username required.
- Receive an autogenerated 6-digit FANZONE ID.
- Join football prediction pools linked to a specific venue.
- Join bar entertainment games and teams.
- Track FET wallet balance, pending rewards, and activity.
- Scan QR codes for venues, tables, pools, games, and TV screens.

FET is a closed-loop engagement and reward point used only inside the FANZONE venue ecosystem. FET is not cash, not crypto trading, not an investment, and cannot be cashed out.

Payments for food and drinks happen outside the app through the venue's own supported payment method. FANZONE does not process card, bank, MoMo, or Revolut payments in the MVP release.
