# FET Reward Terms

## FET Definition

FET is a closed-loop engagement and reward point used inside FANZONE and participating venue experiences.

FET is not cash, not cryptocurrency trading, not a financial instrument, not an investment product, and not withdrawable.

## Earning FET

Users may earn FET from eligible orders, pool settlement, game settlement, promotions, or platform campaigns where enabled.

## Venue Eligibility Rule

To receive FET winnings from a linked game or prediction pool, a user must place at least one qualifying paid order from the linked venue within 2 hours before the scheduled game or pool start time.

Users may join before ordering. Ineligible winners may still appear as winners but do not receive FET settlement.

## Ledger

Every FET movement must be recorded in the ledger. Wallet balances must not be changed without a ledger entry.

## No Negative Balances

Wallets must not go negative. Failed or disputed transactions may be reversed or audited through platform procedures.

## Disputes

Disputed venue payment confirmations, pool results, game results, or reward allocations may be reviewed by FANZONE and the venue.
