# Terms Of Use Draft

## Product Scope

FANZONE provides venue discovery, dine-in ordering support, external payment guidance, closed-loop FET reward points, prediction pools, centralized entertainment games, teams, and venue TV display experiences.

## Accounts

Users sign in with WhatsApp OTP. FANZONE does not require names, usernames, or email addresses. Each user receives an autogenerated 6-digit FANZONE ID.

## Orders And Payments

Orders are linked to venues. Payment is external and handled by the venue through supported methods such as MoMo USSD, Revolut link, cash, card, or other venue instructions. FANZONE does not process payment credentials in the MVP release.

## Venue Confirmation

Venue staff manually marks orders as paid. Manual payment confirmation is auditable and may affect FET reward eligibility.

## Prediction Pools And Games

Prediction pools and games use FET points only. Pools and games are linked to venues. FANZONE may restrict, cancel, settle, reverse, or audit pools and games to enforce eligibility, integrity, and platform rules.

## No Cash-Out

FET cannot be redeemed for cash, withdrawn, sold, traded externally, or treated as an investment product.

## Conduct

Users and venues must not abuse ordering, wallet, pool, game, invite, or screen-control flows. FANZONE may suspend access for fraud, abuse, or policy violations.

## Support

Contact `info@ikanisa.com`.
