## Description

FANZONE connects football fans with sports bars, pubs, lounges, restaurants, and entertainment venues.

Use FANZONE to browse venues, view menus, order food and drinks, follow venue payment instructions, and join venue-linked fan entertainment.

Features:
- WhatsApp OTP login with no email or username required.
- Autogenerated 6-digit FANZONE ID.
- Venue discovery and menu browsing.
- Dine-in ordering with external MoMo USSD, Revolut link, cash, card, or venue payment instructions.
- FET wallet for closed-loop venue engagement points.
- Football prediction pools linked to specific venues.
- Centralized bar games including trivia, music bingo, and song guess sessions.
- Team creation and QR invite flows.
- Eligibility status for FET settlement.

FET is a closed-loop reward point inside FANZONE. FET is not cash, not crypto trading, not an investment product, and cannot be cashed out.

Food and drink payments are handled outside FANZONE by the venue. FANZONE does not process payment cards, bank payments, MoMo transactions, or Revolut payments in the MVP release.
